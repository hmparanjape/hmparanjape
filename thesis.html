Modeling Shape Memory Alloy (SMA) Mechanical Response

A showcase of simulations using a phase field model combining phase transformation and crystal plasticity. Key features of the model include large deformation formulation, martensite CV scale phase transformation and reorientation, crystal plasticity in austenite and a finite element method based implementation.

Paranjape, H. (2014). Modeling of Shape Memory Alloys: Phase Transformation/Plasticity Interaction at the Nano Scale and the Statistics of Variation in Pseudoelastic Performance. (Electronic Thesis or Dissertation). Retrieved from https://etd.ohiolink.edu/
