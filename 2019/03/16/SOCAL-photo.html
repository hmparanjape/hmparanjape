Spring 2019 was about photographing the Bay Area and roadtripping to Southern California.

A Roadtrip to Joshua Tree

One long weekend in the Spring of 2019, I decided to drive to Joshua Tree National Park from Bay Area. I spent two days in the park. On my way back, I stopped in LA for a day to visit The Broad and then drove back to San Jose on US 101. It was a great trip and here are a few photos.

A map of central and southern California. One long weekend, I drove from the Bay Area to Joshua Tree National Park, then to Los Angeles, and finally back home by US 101.
