Progress (6/2/2016)

  1. Implemneted a more efficient numerical procedure for actuation. Two orders of magnitude sppedup.
  2. Tube tension + torsion simulations with varying tube inner diameter: The axial strain for various tubes varies by a small amount. This is anticipated because a constant bias stress is applied to one end of the tube.
  3. Mesh density study: Tension + torsion simulation for each tube inner diameter is run with two or three different mesh densities. The mesh densities are chosen such that there are 1 to 5 elements along the tube wall thickness. The results are mesh sensitive.
