Minimal. Abstract. Geometric. Vibrant.

Over the years, I have explored the combinations of simple geometric shapes and vibrant colors through various means: sculpture, painting, and interactive apps. Recently, I made a series of digital works that explore a similar theme. The goal was relatively simple: generate a series of illustrations that "pop out". The tools were relatively simple too: a few geometric shapes, textures, and lights in Blender 3d modeling software. This page showcases some of my favorite outcomes from this creative process. I ended up making a few prints out of these and they look great! The inspiration for this comes from many sources: well-known minimal/abstract artists, many posts on Behance, and the way some chefs present their dishes.
